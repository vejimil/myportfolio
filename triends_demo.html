Triends — Light Village 2 Demo

A trimmed playable slice of the original light puzzle section. It starts from the light-village-2 spawn, keeps the original collision, transformation, animation, mirror reflection, laser splitting, and light-network puzzle flow, and restarts automatically when you reach Visigi or lose all HP.

Back to Project Page Back to Projects

Controls

  • Astronaut: Arrow keys
  • Ginseng: WASD
  • Transform: R
  • Mirror equip: Right Ctrl / Right ⌘
  • Mirror reflect pose: 0
  • Sunflower fire: While transformed, tap WASD

Goal

Clear the light puzzle path and reach Visigi. The stage resets when you succeed or when HP reaches 0.

What is included

Only the files and systems needed for this section are loaded: the light-village-2 map, the required character sprites, laser/thunder effects, and Visigi.

Everything else from the larger Triends project was intentionally left out of this demo.
