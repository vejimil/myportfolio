Playable Game

Triends — Demo

A browser-playable vertical slice of the light-puzzle section from Triends. It preserves the core transformation, light-routing, mirror reflection, and a fail-and-retry loop while keeping the experience quick to understand and play.

Back to Triends

Play

Playable in browser · focused demo build

Controls

Astronaut

↑ ↓ ← → move

Right Ctrl equip mirror

Right Shift raise mirror

Insam

W A S D move / fire as sunflower

R transform

Goal

  • Use Insam’s light and the astronaut’s mirror to route light through the stage.
  • Reach the goal point to clear the demo.
  • The demo resets automatically after a clear or when HP reaches zero.
