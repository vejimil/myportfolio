Playable Game

Triends — Light Village 2

A browser-playable slice of the original light-village-2 section. It keeps the core collision, transformation, animation, mirror reflection, and light puzzle flow, then restarts from the spawn when you reach Visigi or HP reaches 0.

Back to Triends

Play

Playable in browser · light-village-2 only

Controls

Astronaut

↑ ↓ ← → move

Right Ctrl equip mirror

0 reflect pose

Insam

W A S D move / fire as sunflower

R transform

Goal

  • Use Insam’s light and the astronaut’s mirror to solve the light puzzle.
  • Reach Visigi to clear the demo.
  • The run automatically restarts from the spawn after success or at 0 HP.

Only the files and systems needed for this section are loaded, so the demo stays focused on the original Light Village 2 gimmick.
