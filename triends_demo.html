Triends — Demo

A browser-playable slice of Triends focused on the original light puzzle gimmick. It keeps the core collision, transformation, animation, mirror reflection, and restart flow so the demo stays easy to jump into.

Back to Triends

Play

Playable in browser · focused demo build

Controls

Astronaut

↑ ↓ ← → move

Right Ctrl equip mirror

Right Shift raise mirror

Insam

W A S D move / fire as sunflower

R transform

Goal

  • Use Insam’s light and the astronaut’s mirror to solve the light puzzle.
  • Reach the goal point to clear the demo.
  • The run automatically restarts from the spawn after success or at 0 HP.
